About

I started my career managing 150+ business client accounts, learning what drives revenue, how to read stakeholders, and why relationships are everything. Then I spent nine years in education, leading departments, designing curricula from scratch, and earning a Faculty of the Semester award along the way.

What connects all of it? I've always been building capability in people to create business outcomes. Whether it's designing a training program that actually moves metrics, enabling a team to sell smarter, or partnering with stakeholders to turn strategy into action, I bring a rare combination of business acumen and instructional expertise.

I hold a Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) in Management Economics and Finance from the University of Guelph, and I've spent my career applying that foundation where it matters most: helping people perform at their best while tackling and simplifying complex issues.
